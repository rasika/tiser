Welcome to Interstate Education
The Interstate Education Consultants (Private) Ltd is a company Incorporated by Sri Lankan professionals in order to promote International education and to represent reputed international education institutions, for the first time in Sri Lanka. The purpose of our endeavour is to encourage students to fulfil their educational ambitions in several of disciplines by receiving quality education in dynamic Tertiary Institutions and Universities with established international recognition. more>>

Plan your Future with Interstate
 
Interstate is a one stop centre which provides students with expert counselling in all aspects of overseas university study. Interstate is a regular ‘stop off’ destination for representatives from education institutions in UK, Australia,USA, Canada, New Zealand, India, Singapore, China and Europe
